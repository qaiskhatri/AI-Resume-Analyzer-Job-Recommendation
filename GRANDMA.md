I built a simple website that helps people check their resumes.

You know how people apply for jobs and send their resumes? This tool reads their resume and finds out what skills they have, like Python, Excel, or web development.

Then it:
- Tells them how good their resume is (a score out of 100)
- Suggests what skills they might be missing
- Recommends jobs that match their skills

So instead of guessing, people can understand if their resume is strong or needs improvement.

In simple words, it’s like a smart assistant that reads your resume and tells you:
“Here’s what you’re good at, and here’s what you can improve to get a better job.”
“It’s not perfect, but it gives a quick idea before applying for jobs.”
